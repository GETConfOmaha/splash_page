Gender Equity in Tech: it's not a request

What is GETCONF?

GETConf (Gender Equity in Tech Conference) is a one-day tech conference by women, for everyone on April 26, 2019. Our mission is to amplify and celebrate the voices of women and non-men, and educate and connect people of all genders in the tech community.

GETConf is a day for anyone in tech to learn from both technical and non-technical thought leaders. We aim to strengthen and build the greater tech community.

Talk Selection

The selection committee is reviewing submissions from women and non-men speakers who are passionate about their work and how it relates to the greater tech community. Speakers will be notified of their status by the end of December. Public speaker announcements will start in early 2019.

Want to attend?

GETConf will be April 26, 2019 at Metropolitan Community College's Center for Advanced and Emerging Technology (CAET) in Omaha, Nebraska. The CAET is a regional hub for education, innovation and economic growth, housing the MCC academic data center, simulation and fabrication labs, as well as a business incubator and economic development offices. It truly is a place where business and education thrive together and will be the perfect fit for a day of learning from women and non-men thought leaders in tech.
