Gender Equity in Tech: it's not a request

What is GETCONF?

GETConf (Gender Equity in Tech Conference) is a one-day tech conference by women, for everyone. Our mission is to amplify and celebrate the voices of women and non-men, and educate and connect people of all genders in the tech community.

GETConf is a day for anyone in tech to learn from both technical and non-technical thought leaders. We aim to strengthen and build the greater tech community.

Interested in submitting a talk?

Organizers are looking for women and non-men speakers who are passionate about their work and how it relates to the greater tech community.

  • Do you love a language, library, or framework and want to share why? Submit a talk.

  • Do you have lessons to share about big data, database administration, networking, DevOps, or cybersecurity? Submit a talk.

  • Do you have stories to tell about product development, project management, or design and UI/UX? Submit a talk.

  • Are you the only ___ in the room? Are you an entrepreneur? Have you had an experience in tech others could learn from? Submit a talk!

If you have something for which you are passionate and it relates to tech in any way - even/especially if you aren't sure if you have anything to teach others - then we want to hear from you!
