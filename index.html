After trying to organize a get together of 20 people I soon realized collecting and analyzing everyone's availability for only a few days was a daunting task that could be automated. So I created Availabillity Overlap, where a user can set days and times to poll for and collect availaibility on then get the best day and time for the group as well as which users can attend.
